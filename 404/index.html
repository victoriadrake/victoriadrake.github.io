404

You follow the URL path into the dark woods.

Twists and turns take you past 301 Redirects, posters of lost Internet memes, and a quaint-looking bed and breakfast, unfortunately closed.

You find a small card marked “clue”, and put it in your pocket.

The path does not lead where you had hoped, but at least now you have a clue.

You come across a small sign on the path:

  ____________________
 |        404         |
 |   Page not found   |
 |____________________|
          | |
        \\| |/

Perhaps what you are looking for is elsewhere.
